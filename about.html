Behind The Scenes

I have been making natural products for over ten years and am frequently researching and experimenting to find additional recipes. I became aware of the unnecessary additives being used in everyday products when my mother became sensitive to chemicals.

After doing some of my own research, I found that my fellow citizens in the US are less likely to be aware of the ingredients in their products compared to the citizens in Europe and other developed countries. I see more people developing allergies and being sick more often today versus previous decades (unless you count diphtheria, measles, etc). After reading how easy some recipes are to make at home, it was an easy and fun transition to make natural products.

Other Interests

When I am not at work, class, or studying, I am immersed in a hobby. My main hobby is woodworking and I have made several platform beds and headboards in addition to nightstands, benches, stools, and shelves. My next project is to build a butchers block island for my kitchen.

My other hobbies include leather working, clay sculpting, making bead jewelry and hemp jewelry, sewing, knitting, and jigsaw puzzles (which I glue together and have stacked under my sofa). I also enjoy reading, especially science fiction and urban fantasy, in addition to watching movies of the same genre. Another interest of mine is playing music, specifically the piano, primilary ragtime or classical. I am currently learning banjo and guitar.

When I am not creating something, I am outdoors enjoying nature, at the movies, or playing a video game. Although I used to be an avid World of Warcraft player, I now play primarily console games due to limited free time.
