What is this natural stuff? And Why?

Welcome to Erin's Semester Project. This website was designed to provide recipes for homemade beauty and home care products. We're all becoming more aware of the poisons and chemicals that are in every day products, and healthy alternatives can be pricey and not always readily available depending on where you live. Today's family has little spare time, so it can be hard to think of setting time aside to make anything homemade, and the ingredients can sometimes sound foreign.

These recipes are safe, easy, cost friendly, and they work. What else could you ask for?

Natural Beauty Care Products

From hairspray to chapstick, you can bring out your natural glow with minimal fuss in the kitchen. Curious about what you're breathing in from your hairspray? Unsure of what you're putting on your lips? Fortunately, you don't have to sacrifice beauty for health.

The ingredients used in these recipes are likely already in your home and require only a few minutes to prepare. These products are designed for both men and women, and can easily be customized to your preference using your favorite essential oils and even adjusting the hairspray and gel hold to your preferred strength. Natural Home Care Products

Household cleaners rid your home of unwanted dirt, right? Why replace the dirt with unwanted chemicals and poisons? With these recipes, you can avoid both. They're all safe and effective.

The laundry soap can be used in HE machines and is safe for delicates, yet gets out powerful shit stains. The furniture polish cleans your wood furniture while also nourishing and protecting it. After you use these products, you'll wonder how why more people aren't don't know about these life-saving recipes that actually work!
